About Me

Patty has practiced Yoga for more than 18 years. She came to Yoga after years of “working out” and severe pain related to mis-alignment of the hips and pain in both knees. Through a recommendation by her Physical Therapist she found Yoga, and an amazing mentor and teacher who helped her find healing and strength through her Yoga practice. Since then, she has not looked back. While she spent her career as an executive for big Ad Agencies, Patty finds her soul grounded by Yoga. She is committed to help others find healing through Yoga Therapy and Yoga.

Originally from the Chicago area, Patty lived in Dallas, Texas for years and was fortunate to find her Yoga mentor, teacher and friend, Michelle Andrie. In 2006, Patty completed her training and earned her Yoga teaching certification. She has completed more than 200 hours of teacher training, including the study of the history of Yoga and its true intention, anatomy, the breath and asanas. And she went on to complete her 500 hour certification in Yoga Therapy in 2010 allowing her to work with students one-on-one to find healing in the whole person; mind, body and spirit.

About Yoga Therapy

Yoga Therapy is individual, private sessions to help you address challenges in your body or your life; those things we struggle with most, whether it is physical, spiritual or emotional.

Each session helps identify these challenges in the body; the areas in which we are tight or in pain. Often, this is where we carry our tension and stress ~ creating “blocks” that can cause pain and immobility in body, mind and spirit. And it is from here that we begin to work to create a release.

When the body opens up and releases, we find freedom. Freedom of movement, and along with it, freedom from those things in our life we need to let go…

Yoga Therapy uses body work, guided asanas, and the breath to create a journey to healing.

Each session provides you with a personal practice to do on your own. Because it is through doing your own practice that you will find healing!!

About Bright Spirit Yoga

Bright Spirit Yoga has its roots in the teachings of Michelle Andrie. It combines the discipline of Iyengar Yoga, the breathing practice of Donna Farhi, and the freedom of movement taught by Vanda Scarvelli. Patty believes this combination is the perfect balance, and it has allowed her to explore, grow and heal in body, mind and spirit. Her mission is to bring this experience to others and help them experience life with freedom and joy!
